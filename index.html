Professional Narrative

My journey in the tech industry began with a strong foundation in computer science engineering at the National School for Computer Science in Tunisia. During my studies, I developed a keen interest in AI and machine learning, which led me to pursue internships and projects that allowed me to apply these concepts in real-world scenarios.

At AVIDEA, I had the opportunity to implement end-to-end MLOps pipelines for automotive AI projects, significantly increasing model deployment efficiency. My role involved not only developing deep learning systems for car parts detection but also designing CI/CD pipelines and orchestrating containerized ML solutions.

As an AI Research Intern at IOVision, I conducted extensive research on virtual try-on techniques and contributed to the VITON GitHub repository. This experience honed my skills in deep learning and pre-processing pipelines, allowing me to achieve superior results with the VITON-HR model.

At Takiacademy, I fine-tuned neural network architectures for multilingual chatbots, optimizing model performance and reducing inference time. This project showcased my ability to work with diverse technologies and deliver scalable solutions.

My certifications, including the Certified Kubernetes Application Developer (CKAD) and TensorFlow Developer Certification, reflect my commitment to staying updated with the latest industry standards and best practices.

I am always eager to take on new challenges and collaborate with like-minded professionals to push the boundaries of what AI can achieve. If you are interested in working together or have any questions, feel free to reach out!
